====== Force Field ======
Projects a protective barrier around the wearer. It will absorb HP damage and recharge. If its hitpoints are reduced to 0, the barrier will not recharge naturally until 24 hours have past. 

===== Mk I Force Field =====
**HP** 10, **Fast Heal** 2

===== Mk II Force Field =====
**HP** 20, **Fast Heal** 4

===== Mk III Force Field =====
**HP** 40, **Fast Heal** 8

===== Mk IV Force Field =====
**HP** 80, **Fast Heal** 16
