====== Customer Service ======
Customer Service is the [[Plane]] above [[Higher Space]] and below [[Original Space]]. Most of Customer Service belongs to [[Reality Break]], and is a occupied by a large expanse of electronic equipment built to manipulate the reality of [[Higher Space]]. There are also offices where [[Reality Break]]s customer service respond to requests from customers in [[Higher Space]].

====== History ======
Customer Service was discovered by the [[Stratera]] scientist [[Hodshand Erals]] during the period at which the multiverse was experiencing [[The Warping]].
